I. Create executable
https://www.zacoding.com/en/post/python-selenium-to-exe/
https://stackoverflow.com/questions/49853252/create-a-python-executable-with-chromedriver-selenium

1) cmd-be: pyinstaller --onefile --noconsole shio_claimtracker_calc_v32.py --> létrejön a dist mappa és abban az exe
2) copy chromedriver and necessary files (images, data, etc.) to dist vagy máshova
3) edit spec file: binaries=[('./chromedriver.exe', '.')] -- ha dist mappában van így hivatkozunk rá [(driver path, destination path)]
4) pyinstaller --onefile --noconsole --windowed shio_claimtracker_calc_v32.spec --> létrejön a jó exe

II. Hide chromedriver console when running exe
https://stackoverflow.com/questions/33983860/hide-chromedriver-console-in-python

1) from win32process import CREATE_NO_WINDOW
2) creationflags=CREATE_NO_WINDOW
